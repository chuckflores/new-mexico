  • Riverbend Hot Spring and Resort

    We arrived into the town of Truth or Consequences in the evening after driving 2 hours from White Sands. (We really wanted to see the sun set at White Sands.) We settled into our room at the Riverbend Hot Spring and Resort and then headed to Wal-Mart to pick up some food and wine.

    The town sits on a natural hot spring.

    The Riverbend Resort is a small and tranquil place located on a bend of the Rio Grande. There are babbling fountains in the courtyard.

    Private Hot Springs were available for us to enjoy the hot water in complete peace. It felt so refreshing to go from the cold 45 degree air to the 112 degree hot spring.

    During the second day we explored the town in the afternoon. We had lunch and coffee at (insert place). Then walked up and down the main street.

    In the middle of December the town was completely dead. Most of the shops were closed and everything seemed pretty quiet. So we mostly stuck to the hot springs, not that we were complaining
