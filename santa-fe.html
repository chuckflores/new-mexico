    Arroyo Cafe

    We are coffee fans. We read that Aroyyo Cafe was the spot to get delicious coffee in downtown Santa Fe so we went. We first attempted to get their famous cold brew at 8am before we headed out to Tent Rocks.

    On our third try, success! We drank cold brew espressos while the owner and and a local regular jammed on the guitar and piano. We are so happy we got to experience this cafe!
